About Me

I am Ignacio Villanueva, a spanish front-end developer focused on architecture, front-end performance and mobile and responsive web design.

I co-host WeCodeSign, a weekly podcast about front-end, UX and web design. Co-organize Open Source Weekends, a monthly Open Source meetup and also co-founded FrontFest.es (one of the biggest conferences about front-end development in Spain).

About this site

I use tools and services that make my life much simpler.

This site is built on Jekyll, an static site generator and hosted on Github Pages under the IgnaciodeNuevo / ignaciodenuevo.github.io repository.

The site is written in Sass, and built from scratch without the help of no framework.

The font used is Roboto, and is served via Google Fonts. The photograph used on the homepage’s masthead is of me and was taken by Damián Fernández.

The icons used are Material Design Icons or are maded by me.
