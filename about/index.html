I am Ignacio Villanueva, a spanish frontend developer focused on architecture, frontend performance and mobile and responsive web design.

I co-host WeCodeSign, a weekly podcast about frontend, UX and web design. Co-organize Open Source Weekends, a monthly Open Source meetup and also co-founded FrontFest.es (one of the biggest conferences about frontend development in Spain).

This year and a half, I have discovered my greatest passion.

I dare call myself someone who likes learning, I am also constantly tweeting, writing about the web, graphic design and other topics.

So I work harder every day, learning to be up to date.
